Our Community

RU IT in TLV is a high tech community in Israel for people who have fled from Ukraine, Russia, Belarus and other countries.

The social initiative has been created in response to devastating events in the region. We help tech professionals, start-up founders and investors to onboard into Israeli tech ecosystem - find like-minded people, jobs and projects to invest in.
